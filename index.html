Our Mission

To empower the JavaScript Developer Community in Nairobi and throughout Kenya by creating an environment for them to connect, interact, share ideas and build strong teams. We believe this will empower them and catalyze innovation.

Our Goals

  • To learn something new every time
  • Connect/network/collaborate
  • Raise the web development standards
  • Create a strong community around JavaScript
